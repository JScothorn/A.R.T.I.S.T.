Donated or Research Embryo Fate

  • Embryo donation to another couple may not be possible or may be restricted by law:
    • If no recipient can be found
    • If embryos are not eligible
    • In these instances, after two years the embryo(s) will be discarded by the lab in accordance with laboratory procedures and applicable laws
  • Embryo donation for research may not be possible or may be restricted by law
    • Patient/partner responsibilities:
      • Identify a research group willing to accept your embryos for donation to research
      • Arrange for transfer of embryos to the research facility
      • Storage fees (until the transfer of embryos has been completed)
