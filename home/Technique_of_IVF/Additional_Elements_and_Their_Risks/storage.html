Cryopreserved Embryo Storage

  • Time limitations and fates
    • WSP will only maintain cryopreserved embryos for a period of five years
    • After that time, any cryopreserved embryos must be:
      • Thawed for Frozen Embryo Transfer
      • Donated to another couple
      • Donated to research
      • Discarded
      • Transferred to a long-term storage facility
  • WSP Requirements
    • Patients/couples who have frozen embryo(s) must remain in contact with WSP on a quarterly basis in order to inform the clinic of their wishes as well as to pay fees associated with the storage of their embryo(s)
    • In situations where there is no contact with WSP for a period of one year or fees associated with embryo storage have not been paid for a period of one year and WSP is unable to contact me/us at the last given address and phone number, the embryo(s) will be considered to be abandoned and may be discarded by ARI in accordance with normal laboratory procedures and applicable law
