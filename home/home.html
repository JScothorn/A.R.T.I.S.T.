Our goal is to help you better understand Assited Reproduction before you consent!

Consent forms are aimed to verify your understanding of your medical treatment.
Currently, the Assisted Reproduction consent form exceeds 20 pages in length! Although not all procedures may apply to you, our goal is to give you a better way to understand the following procedures:

  • In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)
  • Micromanipulation (Intracyptoplamic Sperm Injection (ICSI))
  • Assisted Hatchling
  • Embryo Transfer
  • Cryopreservation
